# GapLab Pricing

GapLab is currently distributed as an open‑source proof‑of‑concept under the MIT licence.  In a commercial context the following pricing tiers could be considered.  These tiers are **illustrative only** and not binding.

| Tier             | Intended user             | Features                                          | Pricing model           |
|------------------|---------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Community**    | Hobbyist traders & researchers | Access to the open‑source repo, fixtures and local dashboard. | Free (open source)     |
| **Pro**          | Active intraday traders    | Hosted API with fresh pre‑market data, enhanced memory layer, e‑mail summaries. | Subscription (e.g. $49/mo) |
| **Enterprise**   | Small prop firms & funds  | Dedicated instance, custom integrations, higher data limits, on‑prem deployment. | Custom quote            |

GapLab v1 does **not** include live data feeds, broker integration or execution services.  Pricing would primarily cover operational costs, infrastructure and support.  All paid tiers would emphasise transparency and user control, with manual override and journalling encouraged.
